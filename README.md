Below is a basic breakdown of what's included in the dashboard and some thoughts behind each.

KPI Cards (top row) — at-a-glance snapshots of Total Revenue, Budget Utilization, YTD Support Tickets + avg CSAT, and Gold's YTD price change as a proxy commodity headline.
Sales & Revenue — monthly bar chart with actual vs. target, so users can instantly spot over/underperformance.
Budget vs. Actual — horizontal bars by category, making it easy to see where spend is running hot or lean.
Customer Support — dual-axis line chart tracking ticket volume (left) and CSAT % (right) together, since they often move in opposite directions.
Commodity Prices — multi-line chart for Oil, Gold, Copper, and Natural Gas with toggle buttons so users can isolate or compare any combination. 
I included commodities pricing because these are external signals that contextualize the internal numbers.
